03Why Helix

Three walls keep most people out of sophisticated markets. Helix removes all three.

01 — knowledge

You shouldn't need a glossary

Options, Greeks, implied vol surfaces. Most people quit before they start. Helix asks only what you believe.

Positions shouldn't be a second job

Markets shift; strategies that worked last week stop working. Monitoring, adjusting, rolling — nobody has the hours.

→ Strategies are living and self-adapting.

Screenshots of 500% returns prove nothing. Cherry-picked, unverifiable, sometimes fake.
